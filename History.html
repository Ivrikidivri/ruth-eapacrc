Empowering People With Disabilities

Our History

The East Asia & Pacific Regional Council of Cheshire Homes has a rich legacy of empowering people with disabilities across the region. This page shares our journey, key milestones, and the values that continue to inspire us today.

1950s

Cheshire Homes were founded globally with the vision of providing care and dignity for people with disabilities.

1970s

Expansion into Asia-Pacific, with the establishment of local homes and programs for inclusive development.

1990s

The East Asia & Pacific Regional Council (EAPacRC) was formed to strengthen cooperation and advocacy across the region.

Today

EAPacRC continues to support member homes, advocate for disability rights, and build inclusive communities across East Asia and the Pacific.
